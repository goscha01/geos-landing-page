Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Effective Date: August 8, 2025
Last Updated: August 8, 2025

Introduction

Geos LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our applications, services, and software solutions.

This policy covers all applications and services developed by Geos LLC. Depending on which of our services you use, different sections of this policy may apply to you.

Section 1: General Information Collection & Use

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile information
  • Contact Information: Phone numbers, business contact details
  • Content: Text, images, videos, and other media you upload
  • Preferences: Settings, configurations, and user preferences

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our apps, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address
  • Log Data: IP addresses, browser type, access times, pages viewed, API interactions

Information from Third Parties

We may collect information from third-party platforms and services you connect to our applications, as detailed in the platform-specific sections below.

Section 2: Data Security & Your Rights

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Update: Modify your account information and preferences
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format (where applicable)

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Update your profile settings within our applications
  • Contact us using the information provided below
  • Use account deletion features if available

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Section 3: App-Specific Practices

Social Media Management Apps (Self-Post)

What Self-Post Does: Self-Post is our AI-powered social media management application that automatically creates, schedules, and publishes content to your business social media accounts without manual intervention.

Additional Information We Collect for Self-Post:

  • Social Media Account Access: Authorized connections to your business profiles
  • Content for Posting: Media and text you upload for social media posting
  • Scheduling Information: Automated posting preferences and timing
  • Business Information: Company details and branding preferences
  • AI Content Preferences: Topics, tone, and content guidelines you provide

AI-Generated Content:

  • Our AI system automatically creates social media content based on your preferences and business information
  • Content is generated using industry best practices and your specified guidelines
  • You maintain control over content topics, tone, and posting frequency
  • AI-generated content accuracy is not guaranteed; you are responsible for the content posted to your accounts

Automated Operations:

  • Self-Post operates autonomously after initial setup, potentially for extended periods
  • The system continues posting based on your configured preferences
  • You can modify or halt automated operations at any time through the app settings
  • Emergency stop features are available to pause all automated posting

How We Use Your Information in Self-Post:

  • Content Creation: Generate automated social media posts using AI
  • Account Management: Maintain connections to your social media platforms
  • Publishing: Schedule and post content to your authorized accounts
  • Analytics: Provide insights on post performance and engagement
  • Service Optimization: Improve AI content generation and posting algorithms

Section 4: Platform Integrations

Facebook/Instagram/Meta Platforms

Facebook Permissions We Use:

  • pages_manage_posts: Create, edit, and delete posts on your Facebook Pages
  • pages_read_engagement: Read content and engagement data from your Pages
  • pages_show_list: Access the list of Facebook Pages you manage
  • pages_manage_metadata: Receive notifications about Page activity and update Page settings
  • business_management: Access your Facebook Business Manager accounts and data
  • read_insights: Access analytics and insights data for your Pages

Instagram Permissions We Use:

  • instagram_basic: Access your Instagram account profile and media information
  • instagram_content_publish: Create and publish posts to your Instagram account
  • instagram_manage_insights: Access Instagram analytics and insights data

Google Services (Google My Business)

Google My Business Integration:

  • Business Profile Access: Read and manage your Google My Business listings
  • Post Publishing: Create and publish posts to your business profiles
  • Insights Data: Access performance metrics for your business listings
  • Review Management: Monitor and respond to customer reviews
  • Location Information: Access business location and contact details

LinkedIn Business Profiles

LinkedIn Integration:

  • Company Page Access: Manage posts on your LinkedIn company pages
  • Content Publishing: Create and schedule posts for your business profiles
  • Analytics Access: Retrieve engagement and performance data
  • Follower Information: Access follower demographics and growth metrics

Other Platform Integrations

As we expand our platform integrations, this section will be updated to reflect new social media and business platforms we support.

Important Notes About Platform Integrations:

  • We only access information you explicitly authorize through each platform's permission system
  • You can revoke these permissions at any time through the respective platform's settings
  • We comply with each platform's policies and terms of service
  • Your social media data is used solely for the management features you request

Section 5: How We Share Your Information

We may share your information:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to sharing
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist us
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with legal processes or protect rights and safety
  • Public Information: Content you choose to make public may be visible to other users
