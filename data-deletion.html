Data Deletion Policy

How to request deletion of your account and data from Geos LLC products

Effective Date: April 2, 2026
Last Updated: April 2, 2026
Applies to: All Geos LLC products

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data from any Geos LLC product, you can do so in one of the following ways:

  • In-app: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data.
  • By email: Send a request to info@geos-ai.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account and the name of the product (e.g., ProofPix, Self-Post, ServiceFlow).

What Data Is Deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently delete the following:

  • Your account profile and credentials
  • All user-generated content (photos, projects, posts, documents)
  • Custom settings, preferences, and configurations
  • Team membership and shared access data
  • Usage history and analytics linked to your account
  • Third-party service connections and tokens

Important: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure to export any data you wish to keep before requesting deletion.

Data Retention After Deletion

Once a deletion request is processed, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. The following may be retained:

  • Anonymized analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics (e.g., total photo counts, feature usage trends) that cannot be linked back to your account.
  • Legal obligations: Information required to be retained by law, such as billing records or fraud prevention data, for the minimum period required.
  • Backup systems: Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within 90 days.

Third-Party Services

If you connected external services to any Geos LLC product (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google My Business), deleting your account removes the connection between your account and those services.

Files, posts, or content already exported to or published on external platforms are not affected by the deletion and must be managed directly through those platforms.

We recommend revoking app permissions on each connected platform after account deletion:

  • Google Account Permissions
  • Facebook App Settings
  • Dropbox Connected Apps
  • LinkedIn Permitted Services

Processing Time

  • In-app requests: Processed immediately.
  • Email requests: Processed within 7 business days.

You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Product-Specific Notes

ProofPix

All before/after photo pairs, projects, custom layouts, watermark settings, and team data will be permanently deleted. Photos already exported to Google Drive or Dropbox are not affected.

Self-Post

All scheduled and draft posts, AI content preferences, and social media account connections will be removed. Posts already published to your social media accounts are not affected.

ServiceFlow / LeadBridge / Callio

All customer records, workflows, call logs, and SMS history associated with your account will be deleted. Contacts and messages already delivered are not affected on the recipients' devices.
